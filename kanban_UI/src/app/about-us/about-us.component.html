What is a Kanban Board?

A Kanban board is a visual project management tool that enables teams to visualize work, optimize processes, and boost productivity. Originating from the Japanese manufacturing industry, Kanban has evolved into a powerful methodology embraced by diverse sectors, including software development, marketing, and project management.
