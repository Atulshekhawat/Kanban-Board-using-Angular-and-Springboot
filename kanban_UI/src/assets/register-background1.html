90 Modern and Simple Backgrounds [Edit and Download]

Written by:
Orana Velarde

Feb 21, 2019

Using a simple background can elevate visual content with minimal effort on your part. The variety of possibilities for a subtle and minimal background are almost infinite. You can use photographic details of walls, images of nature, vector designs, seamless patterns, and cloth textures.

Simple backgrounds can be used for any of your visuals; from presentation slides, social media graphics, to flyers, documents and even business cards. The positive aspect of a simple background is that it complements your content without getting in the way. Some minimal backgrounds are subtle but also help convey the message better.

We have put together our favorite simple backgrounds for you to try on future projects. Some of these are available inside the Visme editor while others can be found in Freepik and Creative Market.

The only limit is your imagination.
